    Mone: designed and developed

    When designing mone, I concentrated on where I want to lay one's eye on. The design process took a turn from the usual, the photography marks the genesis of the website. A simplistic design prompt to highlight one's work.

    Although I usually aim for a minimalistic approach. I believe there is a fine line between a good minimalistic design and “the boring”. I began sourcing and absorbing many inspirations leading to even more iterations. Curating colour palettes and establishing grids that enhance the power of white space.

    shaping animations intend to serve a smoother experience on the website. Emphasising what's there, rather than seizing control.

    Nowadays we use so many different devices, in all sizes. I want a website to look good across all all formats, but also behave how you would use it. When hovering and exploring the website on your laptop, you might like your mobile version that can be navigated with just a thumb. Experimenting with larger formats should never compromise the mobile experience. This is why I design responsively, with a mobile-first approach.

    This website has been designed and developed by Iris Simone, with the sole use of HTML and CSS.
