    Iris Simone is an Amsterdam based fashion photographer. With my background in web design & development, and my passion for fashion & photography, I’m exploring how websites can be designed around photography, using fashion photography as the visual core.

    My photographic perspective emerged from ordinary moments like sunlight dancing on water an wind flowing through slivers of fabric. Mone Creative Studio is driven by a passion for fashion & photography and inspired by mundanity.
